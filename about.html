About Vendease

Vendease is a modern procurement platform that helps hotels and restaurants solve issues of quality of products, price hikes by vendors and cashflow. Vendease does this by procuring directly for businesses therefore getting the best prices and quality

In addition, Vendease provides data driven market and consumer insights to help businesses reduce waste and optimize profits.
